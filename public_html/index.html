Decipher

Decipher or level one is the pen paper test in which participants will come in a team which will have four members. This round will be of 60 marks.
This test will be comprised of three sections:

  • Section A: Ten Questions will be asked of one marks.
  • Section B: Five Questions will be asked of four marks (Negative marking -2 Marks)
  • Section C: Three Questions will be asked of ten marks (Subjective Type)

Mechtricity

Mechtricity or level two will be comprised of two parts.

  • Part 1: In this round you have to make a mechanism and fit the gears in a such a way that it works
  • Part 2: In this round you have to complete some electrical ciruits

Splash On

Splash On or level three will be the most intresting round.In this round you have to fabricate a bot that could travel along a specified length of track and they will shoot some of the targets.
